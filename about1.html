About Us

U3A is the University of the Third Age, which is the age of active retirement, coming after the age of youth and work and home making. University is a loose term - there are no academic requirements for membership, no awards, and no exams!

U3A is a worldwide self-help organisation promoting learning for personal enjoyment and satisfaction.

Research shows that keeping the brain active, doing interesting things and making new friends are essential for helping older people to maximise their chances of independence, and this is what U3A does very effectively. U3A is making a very substantial contribution to society by helping its members to remain healthy and active for longer, thus lessening the strain on our already overstretched health system.

  • Learning Philosophy

    At U3A, members learn together in a collegiate culture of like-minded peers who share a common goal of learning for pleasure. Naturally, this forges friendships and networks which reach out to other contacts in our age group and which address many of the risks to general health we all face as we age.

  • Management and Administration

    U3A Brisbane is managed by a 10-person volunteer Management Committee elected annually at the AGM, with sub-committees responsible for specific areas. They are ably supported by a large number of volunteer Coordinators and administration staff. All classes are conducted by volunteer tutors.

  • Classes

    We have over 4000 members and offer more than 290 courses each term, all of these at a low cost. Our City Campus classes are held at Ground Floor, 97 Creek Street, Brisbane and Level 5, 232 Adelaide St, Brisbane.
