Get Started

Become a member

If you would like to join U3A and enjoy all the benefits of being a member it offers, just follow the steps below. This simple process will open up a world of new friendships and experiences for you.

5 Simple Steps to Joining U3A


Step 1

Click on the link below and print off the New Membership form
APPLICATION-FOR-MEMBERSHIP-2020-1

Step 2

Write the requested information by printing, and include a contact name and telephone number.

Step 3

Read the fine print! Then sign the form.

Step 4

Take the completed and signed form into U3A during office hours, together with the required membership fee. You can pay by cash, cheque or EFTPOS.

Step 5

Receive your badge and start enjoying your membership. Welcome aboard.


Membership Fees

  • $20.00 per person for a full year.
  • $15.00 per person when joining for terms 3 and 4
  • $10.00 per person when joining for term 4 only


    • Reciprocal Membership

    If you are a paid-up member of another U3A group (anywhere) you are entitled to join as a Reciprocal Member. The cost is $10.00. When joining U3A Redcliffe you will need to show your membership card or receipt from the other U3A group.
