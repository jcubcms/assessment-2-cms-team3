About Us

U3A is the University of the Third Age, that is the age of active retirement,

it comes after the age of youth and work and homemaking. University is an easy term - there are no exams and academic requirements for membership. U3A is a worldwide self-helping organization promoting learning for personal enjoyment and wellbeing.

  • Keeping the brain active,doing interesting things,
  • connecting new friends are essential for helping older people to maximize their chances of independence,and this is what U3A does very effectively
  • U3A is doing a very substantial contribution to society by helping its members to remain healthy and active for longer.
